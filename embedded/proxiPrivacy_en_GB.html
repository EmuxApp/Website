Information we may collect about you

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  1. Information you give us (Submitted Information)
    You may give us information about you by filling in forms on Proxi or by corresponding with us by email or otherwise. This includes information you provide when you register with Proxi. The information you give us may include your name, username, password, profile photo, and any other information you provide.
  2. Information we collect about you
    With regard to each of your visits to Proxi, we may automatically collect the following information about you:
    • Technical information, including the type of mobile device you use, mobile network information, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile browser you use (Device Information)
    • Details of your use of proxi (Log Information)
    • Location information ─ We use GPS technology to determine your current location. Some of our location-enabled services require your personal data for the feature to work. If you wish to use the particular feature, you will be asked to consent to your data being used for this purpose. You can enable or disable location services when you use our service at any time through your mobile device settings (Location Information)
    • Statistical data ─ Proxi may collect information about user demographics through Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting. This information will only be used for internal reporting and not shared with third parties
    • Activity data ─ Proxi is designed to track your location and activity. We may collect your activity data (for example location and distance) when you employ features in Proxi that are designed to capture your activity data
    • Contact information ─ You may have the option of providing contact information to us in order to get help from us. We will not collect this information unless you specifically elect to share this information with us
    • Data collected from Software Development Kits (SDK) ─ Proxi uses SDKs to distinguish you from other users. This helps us collect information about how you are using Proxi so we can ensure we are providing you with a good experience when using Proxi and allows us to improve Proxi in future. By downloading Proxi, you are agreeing to our use of SDKs
